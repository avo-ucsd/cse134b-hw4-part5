Tears of the Kingdom takes everything that made Breath of the Wild great and expands on it in meaningful ways.

The new building mechanics add a whole new dimension to puzzle solving. Being able to fuse items together creates endless possibilities for creative solutions.

The sky islands provide a fresh perspective on Hyrule, and the depths add a sense of mystery and danger that keeps exploration exciting.

Combat feels refined with the new abilities, and boss fights are more challenging than ever.

Overall, this is a masterpiece that sets a new standard for open world games.
